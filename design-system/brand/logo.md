# Logo

The Back Market logo has two lockup formats, both in black.

| File | Format | Use |
|------|--------|-----|
| `brand/logo-horizontal.svg` | Horizontal | Default — landscape layouts |
| `brand/logo-stacked.svg` | Stacked | Portrait layouts |
| `icons/svg/IconLogoBackMarket.svg` | Symbol only | Space-limited contexts (app icon, favicon, avatar) |

The horizontal and stacked lockups are interchangeable — use whichever fits the layout orientation better.

---

## Clearspace

Always maintain a minimum clearspace around the logo. The unit is defined proportionally from the logo's own dimensions (referred to as "x" in the brand guidelines). No other element, text, or edge may enter this zone.

The same clearspace rule applies to all versions including the symbol.

---

## Minimum sizes

| Version | Digital | Print |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Stacked | 50px height | 15mm width |
| Horizontal | 25px height | 25mm width |
| Back Symbol | 32px height | 4mm width |

Never render the logo below these thresholds.

---

## Colour

The logo files are black. Two colour applications are permitted:

- **Black** — on light backgrounds within the brand colour palette
- **White** — on dark/inverse backgrounds within the brand colour palette

When placing the logo on a dark background, recolour the SVG fill to white (`#ffffff`). No other colours are permitted — the logo must never be tinted, adjusted to any intermediate colour, or placed on a background outside the brand colour palette.

---

## When to use the Back Symbol alone

The Back Symbol (the double-chevron square mark) is reserved for space-limited contexts where the full lockup cannot fit:

- App icon
- Favicon
- Social media avatars

The Back Symbol must never be combined with the wordmark — use one or the other.

---

## Prohibited uses

- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not recolour the logo — black and white only
- Do not place the logo on backgrounds outside the brand colour palette
- Do not place the logo on top of photography or imagery
- Do not modify, alter, or add effects to the logo
- Do not obstruct the logo with other elements
- Do not violate the clearspace zone
